About CSIRAC:

CSIRAC, Australia's first computer, as well as the fourth computer
in the world, was designed and built in Australia.It is also the only
intact first-generation computer surviving anywhere in the world.

As the first computer in Australia, CSIRAC played an important role
in power supply, atom arrangement, loan payment calculation, etc.
In 2000, CSIRAC found its permanent home in Melbourne Museum and has
not been operated since its shutdown. However, many of the programs
that ran on it have been preserved,an emulator has been written for it.

This webpage aims to enable more people to experience the use of the
emulator in modern times. By inputting instructions and data, users
are able to see how the emulator works.

Enjoy your CSIRAC trip!
